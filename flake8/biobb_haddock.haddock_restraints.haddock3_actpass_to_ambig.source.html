Accepted formats: txt (edam:format_2330), dat (edam:format_2330), in (edam:format_2330), pass (edam:format_2330).
24 input_actpass2_path (str): Path to the second input HADDOCK active-passive file containing active (in the first line) and passive (second line) residues.
